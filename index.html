About Me

I also have strong experience with databases like MySQL, MSSQL, and PostgreSQL, along with a basic working knowledge of Data Engineering. Moving forward, my focus will be on AI and data science-related technologies as part of my Master’s degree.
You can find me as a friendly, responsible and co-operative team member. I am ready for new challenges and eager to work with teams that appreciate innovation and growth.
